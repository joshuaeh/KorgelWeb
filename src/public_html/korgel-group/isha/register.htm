Conference Registration

To register for the Conference, please visit this link: http://www.utexas.edu/ce/tcc/events/ISHA-2013/.

The online registration offers either the option of using their secure credit card payment system or the option of sending in your physical payment. Registration costs go to help fund the conference and are $650 for regular participants and $250 for students. This price does not include hotel accomodations but does include breakfast, lunch and all day coffee/snack breaks from the 14th through 16th. If you register but are unable to attend the conference, we will provide you with a refund of 75% of your registration cost if you cancel by 12/1, 50% of the cost if you cancel between 12/1 and 12/15 and 0% after that. There is no penalty for moving your registration fee to another attendee and this can be done at any time.

ISHA 2013 will take place at the AT&T Conference Center where we have set aside a large number of rooms for conference attendees. In addition to the on-site rooms, we have secured rooms nearby at the OMNI Downtown Austin and the Stephen F. Austin InterContinental Hotel. Reservation details for each hotel are provided below.
